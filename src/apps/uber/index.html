  • What is CookIt?
    CookIt is an on-demand service platform where users can request custom made meals from CookIt chefs.
  • Why should I use CookIt?
    Sometimes we crave different kinds of food. CookIt allows users to request meals of all kinds. We offer you a ease of use, and a unique, one-of-a-kind experience with the chefs.
  • What services are offered?
    Premium: Ingredients and service will be provided by the chef.
    Standard: Service will be provided by the chef.
  • What are the costs?
    As of now, we do not have a defined business model. However, costs will be based on a number of factors, such as time, amount of food, kinds of food, and traffic.
  • What kinds of food do you have?
    We have a variety of food, including but not limited to: American, Asian (Chinese, Thai, Japanese, Korean, Indian), Mediterranean, French, Italian, Persian.
